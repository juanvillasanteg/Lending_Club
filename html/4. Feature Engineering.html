4.2 Mean encoding¶

To convert categorical variables into numerical ones, facilitating the construction of a predictive model we use the mean coding, a method which use the objective variable as the basie for generating the new encoded variable.

We will apply mean encoding on the following variables:

  1. addr_state
  2. emp_length
  3. purpose
